# Latest News: India — March 2026

## Finance Bill 2026 Passed

The Lok Sabha passed the **Finance Bill 2026** with 32 government amendments on March 25, completing a key step in the budget process for 2026-27:

- Total proposed expenditure of **₹53.47 lakh crore** (a 7.7% increase).
- Capital expenditure set at **₹12.2 lakh crore**.
- Fiscal deficit projected at **4.3% of GDP**.
- Finance Minister **Nirmala Sitharaman** announced a **$70 billion investment in datacenters** with tax holidays to boost local job creation.
- The bill now moves to the Rajya Sabha for approval.

## Strait of Hormuz Access Secured

Iran granted India and four other "friendly nations" — China, Russia, Iraq, and Pakistan — passage through the strategically vital **Strait of Hormuz** for commercial shipping:

- The strait handles roughly **20% of global oil and LNG** traffic.
- Iran has largely blocked the waterway amid its ongoing conflict with Israel and the US.
- India has been engaged in diplomatic efforts to end the West Asia conflict and ensure uninterrupted energy flow through the critical shipping lane.

## Biometric SIM Verification System (BIVS)

The Centre is fast-tracking a **Biometric Identity Verification System** across all telecom service providers to curb digital fraud:

- Targets digital arrest scams and fraudulent SIM issuance.
- SIM cards will be capped at **nine per person**.
- Real-time identity checks will be implemented within a **nine-month rollout** window.

## Political Developments

- Congress criticized the Modi government's diplomacy, claiming India has been turned from a "broken country" into a "broker country," citing Pakistan's reported role as a mediator in West Asia peace talks.
